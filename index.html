OUR GUESTS

Major General S.K.Vidyarthi, AVSM, SM General Officer Commanding (UM & G)

Major General S.K. Vidyarthi, AVSM, SM, General officer commanding (UM&G) an NDA alumnus, served 65 Engineer Bridge Regiment for 35+ years. He excelled on the Line of Control, in High Altitude Areas, Line of Actual Control, and Desert & Semi-desert sectors. A Sena Medal recipient, he commanded in diverse roles, including a Mountain Brigade in Arunachal Pradesh and an Infantry Division in the Western sector, in addition to key staff positions at various Army headquarters.

Brigadier Suhaas M Kulkarni

Brigadier Suhaas M Kulkarni is an alumnus of Indian Military Academy, Dehradun. He was commissioned in the Corps Of Electrical & Mechanical Engineers of Indian Army. In his 34 years of service he has gained experience of serving in various units from Siachin to Gopalpur-on sea and from Tawang opposite China Border to Jodhpur on the West. During Kargil operations, he served in Sunderbani sector along Pir Panjal ranges.

Pravin Muley

Praveen Muley is a seasoned professional with 25+ years of diverse experience in Global Engineering, IT Infrastructure, Project Management, and Telecom Carrier Wholesale/Enterprise Business Management. Holding an MBA and a B.E. in Electronics, he possesses certifications in Cloud Computing, Blockchain, ITIL, PMP, and more. Currently the CEO & Director at Microscan Communications, he excels in blending tech expertise with business acumen, offering services in IT, Cloud Migration, Cybersecurity, and more. He has held key roles at Infosys, MainOne, and Vodafone India Ltd, earning recognition as a leader in complex technical environments.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How can one register for the cybersecurity event?

To register for the event, simply visit our registration page and follow the easy steps to secure your spot. You can also find our registration desk at IT Square during college time.

What is the dress code for the event?

There is a formal attire requirement only for the MUN event, otherwise we encourage you to dress comfortably.

Is this event open to all skill levels?

Yes, our cybersecurity event welcomes participant of all skill levels, from beginners to experts. We offer a range of activities and workshops suitable for everyone.

What is the event schedule?

You can find the detailed event schedule on the "Agenda" page of our website. It includes information about keynote speakers, breakout session, and networking opportunities.

Is there a fee to attend the cybersecurity event?

Our event offers both free and paid registration options. Please review the registration page for pricing details and what each option includes.
